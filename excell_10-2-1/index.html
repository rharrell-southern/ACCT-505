Wong Corporation


Part One

On January 1, Wong Corporation had 55,000 shares of par common stock issued and outstanding. During the year, the following transactions occurred.

Apr. 1 Issued 10,000 additional shares of common stock for $12 per share.
Jun. 15 Declared a cash dividend of $1.30 per share to stockholders of record on June 30.
July 10 Paid the $1.30 cash dividend.
Sept. 1 Issued 5,000 additional shares of common stock for $14 per share.
Sept. 30 Declared a cash dividend on outstanding shares of $1.50 per share stockholders of record on December 31.

Instructions:
  1. Prepare the entries, if any, on each of the three dates that involved dividends.
